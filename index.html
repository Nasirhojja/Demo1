About

Lynda Weeden is the Company’s owner and President.Lynda brings more than 40 years of practical bookkeeping and office administration experience, as well a huge heart for making sure that all the pieces of your puzzle go together perfectly. From the first meeting with our clients through handling the details of every client’s individual business plan, Lynda makes sure that everything runs smoothly and stays on schedule. Lynda has been taking care of small businesses and law firms her entire career. She brings a wealth of knowledge and a broad base of skills to bear for each client she works with. Lynda is a Florida native, born in Tampa. She was raised in Atlanta and returned to Jacksonville in 1985. She resides in the Jacksonville neighborhood of Mandarin with her husband, Charles, and their four-legged kid, Zeus, a rescued Great Dane.
